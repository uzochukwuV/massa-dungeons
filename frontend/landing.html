Game Features

Experience the next generation of blockchain gaming

Character Classes

Choose from Warrior, Assassin, or Mage. Each class has unique abilities, equipment slots, and combat strategies.

PVP Battles

Engage in turn-based combat with real stakes. Execute attacks, cast spells, and use special abilities to defeat opponents.

Prediction Markets

Bet on battle outcomes. Create prop bets on specific events. Earn rewards from accurate predictions and market liquidity.

Autonomous Bots

Smart contracts automatically resolve battles, manage tournaments, and settle prediction markets. No manual intervention needed.

Skills & Abilities

Unlock powerful abilities: healing, damage buffs, shields, critical strikes, and more. Build your perfect warrior strategy.

Tournaments & Rankings

Compete in tournaments, climb leaderboards, and earn achievements. Prize pools distributed automatically via smart contracts.
